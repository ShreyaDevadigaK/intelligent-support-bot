ACMESAAS REFUND POLICY
Version 2.1 | Last Updated: January 2025
============================================

SECTION 1 — ELIGIBILITY FOR REFUNDS

1.1 Full Refund (100%)
Customers are eligible for a full refund if:
- Cancellation is requested within 14 days of initial purchase (new customers only)
- Service was completely unavailable for more than 48 consecutive hours due to our fault
- Customer was charged for a plan they never activated

1.2 Partial Refund (Pro-rated)
Customers are eligible for a pro-rated refund if:
- Downgrade occurs mid-billing cycle
- Annual plan is cancelled after 14-day window but before 6 months
- Duplicate charge occurred — the extra charge is refunded 100%

1.3 No Refund Situations
Refunds are NOT issued for:
- Monthly plans cancelled after 3 days of the billing date
- Annual plans cancelled after 6 months
- Accounts terminated due to Terms of Service violations
- Unused features or integrations the customer chose not to use
- Third-party add-on costs (Zapier, Twilio, Stripe fees)

SECTION 2 — HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND

2.1 Submission Methods
- Email: billing@acmesaas.com (response within 4 business hours)
- Live chat: Available Monday–Friday 9am–6pm IST
- Support portal: support.acmesaas.com/refund

2.2 Required Information
When requesting a refund, provide:
- Account email address
- Invoice number (format: INV-XXXXXX)
- Reason for refund
- Bank account details for manual refunds (if applicable)

2.3 Processing Time
- Credit/Debit card refunds: 5–7 business days
- UPI refunds: 2–3 business days
- Bank transfer refunds: 7–10 business days
- Wallet credits (if customer prefers): Within 24 hours

SECTION 3 — BILLING ERROR REFUNDS

3.1 Duplicate Charges
If a customer is charged more than once for the same billing cycle:
- The duplicate amount is refunded automatically within 2 business days
- Customer receives a confirmation email with refund reference number
- No manual request is needed — our system detects duplicates automatically

3.2 Wrong Plan Charges
If a customer is charged for a higher plan than subscribed:
- Full difference is refunded within 2 business days
- Customer is notified by email
- Account is corrected automatically

3.3 Unauthorized Charges
If a customer reports an unauthorized charge:
- Account is frozen immediately pending investigation
- Investigation completed within 72 hours
- Full refund issued if unauthorized charge is confirmed

SECTION 4 — ESCALATION

4.1 If Refund is Denied
Customers can escalate to billing@acmesaas.com with subject line "REFUND ESCALATION"
Response guaranteed within 1 business day.

4.2 Executive Escalation
For unresolved disputes exceeding 7 days, customers may contact:
escalations@acmesaas.com — handled by Customer Success VP directly.
