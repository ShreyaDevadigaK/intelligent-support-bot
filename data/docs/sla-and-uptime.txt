ACMESAAS SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
Version 3.0 | Effective: January 2025
============================================

SECTION 1 — UPTIME GUARANTEE

1.1 Uptime Commitment by Plan

   Starter Plan:   99.5% monthly uptime
   Growth Plan:    99.9% monthly uptime
   Business Plan:  99.95% monthly uptime
   Enterprise Plan: 99.99% monthly uptime

1.2 How Uptime is Calculated
   Uptime % = ((Total minutes in month − Downtime minutes) / Total minutes) × 100
   Scheduled maintenance windows are EXCLUDED from downtime calculation.

1.3 Scheduled Maintenance
   - Scheduled every 2nd Sunday between 2:00 AM and 4:00 AM IST
   - Advance notice: 72 hours via email and status page
   - Emergency maintenance: Minimum 30-minute notice via status page

SECTION 2 — SERVICE CREDITS FOR DOWNTIME

2.1 Credit Schedule
   If monthly uptime falls below the guaranteed level:

   Starter Plan:
   - 99.0% to 99.4%: 10% bill credit
   - 98.0% to 98.9%: 25% bill credit
   - Below 98.0%:    50% bill credit

   Growth Plan:
   - 99.5% to 99.8%: 10% bill credit
   - 99.0% to 99.4%: 25% bill credit
   - Below 99.0%:    50% bill credit

   Business Plan:
   - 99.9% to 99.94%: 10% bill credit
   - 99.5% to 99.89%: 25% bill credit
   - Below 99.5%:     50% bill credit

   Enterprise Plan:
   - 99.95% to 99.98%: 10% bill credit
   - 99.9% to 99.94%:  25% bill credit
   - Below 99.9%:      50% bill credit

2.2 Maximum Credit
   Total service credits cannot exceed 50% of monthly bill in any single month.
   Credits are applied to next invoice, not paid as cash refunds.

2.3 How to Claim SLA Credits
   - Submit claim within 30 days of the incident
   - Email: sla@acmesaas.com with subject "SLA CREDIT CLAIM"
   - Include: Account email, incident date, incident reference number
   - Claims reviewed within 5 business days

SECTION 3 — INCIDENT RESPONSE TIMES

3.1 Severity Definitions and Response Commitments

   SEV-1 (Critical) — Complete outage, all users affected
   - Detection target:  5 minutes (automated monitoring)
   - First response:    15 minutes
   - Status update:     Every 30 minutes
   - Resolution target: 2 hours

   SEV-2 (High) — Major feature unavailable, >50% users affected
   - First response:    30 minutes
   - Status update:     Every 1 hour
   - Resolution target: 4 hours

   SEV-3 (Medium) — Partial degradation, workaround available
   - First response:    2 hours
   - Status update:     Every 4 hours
   - Resolution target: 24 hours

   SEV-4 (Low) — Minor issue, cosmetic, single user
   - First response:    1 business day
   - Resolution target: 5 business days

3.2 Status Page
   Live status, incident history, and maintenance schedule:
   status.acmesaas.com
   Subscribe for real-time alerts via email or Slack.
