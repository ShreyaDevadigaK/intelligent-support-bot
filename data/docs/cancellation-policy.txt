ACMESAAS CANCELLATION POLICY
Version 1.8 | Last Updated: January 2025
============================================

SECTION 1 — HOW TO CANCEL

1.1 Self-Service Cancellation
Customers can cancel anytime from:
- Dashboard → Account Settings → Subscription → Cancel Plan
- No phone call required. Cancellation is instant.

1.2 Cancellation via Support
If self-service is unavailable:
- Email: cancel@acmesaas.com
- Live chat with subject "Cancel My Account"
- Response and confirmation within 2 business hours

1.3 What Happens After Cancellation
- Access continues until end of current billing period
- No further charges after cancellation date
- Data is retained for 90 days post-cancellation (then permanently deleted)
- Customer receives cancellation confirmation email immediately

SECTION 2 — MONTHLY PLAN CANCELLATIONS

2.1 Timing
- Cancellations take effect at end of current billing month
- Example: Cancel on March 15 → Access until March 31 → No April charge

2.2 Refund Eligibility
- Monthly plans: No refund after day 3 of billing cycle
- If cancelled within 3 days: Eligible for full refund (first time only)

SECTION 3 — ANNUAL PLAN CANCELLATIONS

3.1 Within 14 Days (Free Trial Window)
- Full refund, no questions asked
- Processed within 5–7 business days

3.2 Between 14 Days and 6 Months
- Pro-rated refund for unused months
- Formula: (Months remaining / 12) × Annual fee paid
- Example: Cancel after 3 months on ₹12,000/year plan → Refund = (9/12) × ₹12,000 = ₹9,000

3.3 After 6 Months
- No refund issued
- Access continues until annual period ends

SECTION 4 — TEAM AND ENTERPRISE PLAN CANCELLATIONS

4.1 Team Plans (2–10 seats)
- 30-day written notice required
- Email: enterprise@acmesaas.com
- Pro-rated refund calculated by support team

4.2 Enterprise Plans (11+ seats)
- 60-day written notice required per contract terms
- Governed by individual Master Service Agreement (MSA)
- Contact your dedicated Customer Success Manager

SECTION 5 — PAUSE INSTEAD OF CANCEL

5.1 Plan Pause Option
Before cancelling, customers can pause their subscription:
- Available for monthly plans only
- Pause duration: 1 to 3 months maximum
- Cost: ₹199/month to hold account and data
- All data and settings preserved during pause

5.2 How to Pause
Dashboard → Account Settings → Subscription → Pause Plan
Or contact support@acmesaas.com

SECTION 6 — REACTIVATION AFTER CANCELLATION

- Reactivate anytime within 90 days at same plan pricing
- After 90 days: Data deleted, reactivation at current pricing
- Reactivate at: app.acmesaas.com/reactivate
